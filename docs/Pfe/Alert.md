
## Function: `Lib.Alert`
- Plays an alert
> Client & Server, you can also use Lib.Script.Alert 
### Parameters:
- `Message` (string): Text sent to the alert.
- `color` (Color3): Color of the text.
- `length` (Number): Length of the alert
### Returns:
- `nothing`
## Example
```lua
Lib:Alert("Hello testing gayman67",Color3.new(1, 1, 0),5)
```

> Added in 1.0.0
